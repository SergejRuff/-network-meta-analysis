
## Notation

- Meta-analysis graph -> undirected graph
 - undirected graph -> pair (V,E)
 - V -> set of vertices
 - n -> vertices
 - E -> vertices n in V connected with edges.
 - w : E  -> R⁺ -> weight function where 
 - w -> weight of edge e in Subset E and w is always positive >0
 - e -> edge in Subset E
